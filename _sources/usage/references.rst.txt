Glossary
--------

The glossary directive allows you to define terms and link to them easily throughout your documentation.

.. glossary::

    Sphinx
        A tool that makes it easy to create intelligent and beautiful documentation.

    RST
        reStructuredText, the markup language used by Sphinx.

You can link to a term like :term:`Sphinx` anywhere.

Footnotes
---------

Footnotes are useful for adding side notes or clarifications.

Here is a reference to a footnote [1]_.

.. [1] This is the content of the footnote.

Citations
---------

Citations are similar to footnotes but are used for bibliographic references.

See [Ref2025]_ for more details.

.. [Ref2025] Author, A. (2025). *The Art of Documentation*. Photon Press.
